Features

Everything a coach
and athlete need.

Built for the reality of serious coaching — not another generic workout tracker.

Custom Programs

Build from 1,300+ exercises. Per-day splits, warm-ups, supersets, rest timers — full control over every detail.

Live Progress

Every set, rep, and personal record tracked in real time. Trainers see exactly what their athletes are doing.

Built-In Chat

Real-time encrypted messaging. Share form checks, celebrate wins, ask questions — all in one thread.

Meal Planning

Macro-tracked meal plans with per-ingredient breakdowns. Calorie sliders for logging actual intake.

AI Workouts

Generate intelligent workout plans in seconds. Set parameters, let AI build, then customize to perfection.

Offline-First

Full offline support with smart background sync. Log workouts at any gym — even with zero signal.
