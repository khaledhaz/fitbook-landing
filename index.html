Features

Everything a coach
and athlete need.

Built for the reality of serious coaching — not just another workout tracker.

Custom Programs

Trainers build workout plans from 1,300+ exercises. Per-day splits, warm-ups, supersets — full control.

Live Progress

Every set, rep, and PR tracked automatically. Trainers see trainee progress in real time.

Built-In Chat

Real-time messaging between coach and athlete. No more WhatsApp chaos or lost context.

Meal Planning

Full meal plans with macro breakdowns, calorie tracking sliders, and per-meal ingredient lists.

AI Generation

Generate complete workout plans with AI in seconds. Fine-tune parameters, then customize.

Offline-First

Full offline support with smart sync. Log workouts anywhere — even without signal.

How It Works

From zero to first rep
in under 3 minutes.

01

Create your profile

Five beautiful steps: about you, body metrics, goals, nutrition habits, and final details. Takes under 2 minutes.

02

Connect with your coach

Find a trainer or invite yours with a shareable link. One tap to connect — they build your program from there.

03

Follow your program

Daily workouts, meals, and supplements on your schedule. Log every set with one tap. Track macros with sliders.

04

See real results

Streaks, personal records, body composition trends. Your trainer adjusts your program as you progress.
